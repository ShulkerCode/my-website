Why <?>

Posted by S4qC • June 3, 2025

Why did we choose <?> as our logo?

Well… it’s kind of a funny story 😄

Most programming logos use the classic </> symbol — you’ve probably seen that around a lot. But here’s the twist: every time I (S4qC) try to type a slash (/), I accidentally press Shift and type a question mark (?) instead. It happens all the time — like muscle memory gone wrong.

One day, I looked at <?> and thought, “You know what? This actually looks kinda cool.” So instead of fixing the mistake… we made it our thing!
