Why <?>

Published on June 3, 2025

Why did we choose <?> as our logo?

Well… it’s kind of a funny story

Most programming logos you see out there use the classic </> symbol — you’ve probably spotted that one a million times. But here’s the twist: every time I (S4qC) try to type a slash (/), I end up accidentally holding Shift and typing a question mark (?) instead. Seriously, it happens like 90% of the time. Muscle memory, I guess?

One day, while messing around, I saw <?> and thought, “Hey, wait a minute… this actually looks kinda cool.” Instead of fixing my mistake… I embraced it! And now it’s our official logo. We think it’s unique, a little quirky, and honestly — it fits our vibe perfectly.

So yeah, that’s the story. A mistake turned into a symbol. Welcome to ShulkerCode 😄
